Main Character

Meet the main character! Her name is Mya, she has plenty of hidden talents, skills, and abilities that the player can discover while playing the game! Mya is the character the player will play as throughout the storyline.

Side Characters

Unlike Mya, everyone else in the village cannot see the magic. There are a few side characters that the player will interact with to restore the magic, however the villagers' lack of magic knowledge creates a challenge.

Villains and Antagonists

Throughout the long quest to save the world, the player will be faced with many villagers trying to stop them. The first of them being the blacksmith, an older man who has struggled with misfortune his whole life.

World Map Preview

The world of Rosewood is anything but small. This world has a variety of biomes, landscapes, and breathtaking views for the player to heal and discover.
